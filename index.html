Warning: not to scale! Each building is hand drawn and imperfect. See the (teeny tiny) layout key on the back side of the postcard for the full list of included landmarks. Suggested donation of $7 to @maehubel but pay what you can! All proceeds will go towards charity (ImmDef) to support our neighbors to be safe in their own home.

This started as a morning ritual to get me out in fresh air & mindful presence before the workday monopolized the rest of my day's light. It became a mission to document a neighborhood that has proven to be surprisingly walkable despite the insistent car-centricity of SoCal. I am fond of this neighborhood and grateful for the home it has given me :)
